Meet the Outlaws

Guru and Sterring - Paradise Park's most dangerous duo

ACTION STORIES: WELCOME TO PARADISE PARK

A Manga Series Set in the Wild Streets of Azania

Welcome to Paradise Park — a wild town in the heart of Azania, where roaring lions serve as police patrols, people ride springboks instead of cars, and nature isn't just part of life... it runs it.

In this rough-and-ready town, crime runs deep — but two outlaws are about to flip the rules on their head.

Meet Guru

The sly, smooth-talking thief who only robs other thieves. Once a top member of the notorious Triple 3 Gang, he was betrayed and left to rot in prison. Now he's out — sharper, smarter, and seriously out for revenge. Guru knows every back alley, every gang leader's weakness, and every loophole in the system — and he plans to hit the Triple 3 where it hurts: their pockets.

At his side is Sterring

Ex-cop, ex-con, and an all-out loose cannon. He doesn't care about revenge. He wants justice, and if that means kicking in doors and going full Rambo with his modified zebra-striped shotgun? So be it. While Guru schemes, Sterring charges head-first into the chaos, ready to break bones and blow stuff up.

Together, they're not heroes. They're not villains either. They're something in-between — guns-for-hire with a moral code (and a price tag).

If the people of Paradise Park want protection, they gotta pay up. Because Guru and Sterring don't save anyone for free.

But trouble's brewing: the Triple 3 Gang wants Guru's head, and a mysterious new power is rising in the streets — one that threatens to tip Paradise Park into total war.

Can these two mismatched renegades survive the chaos they're unleashing?
Or will Paradise Park chew them up and spit them out?

THIS & THAT: NETBALL ADVENTURES

Friendship. Rivalry. Netball. Welcome to the real game.

Jade Petersen is 15, proudly coloured, confidently chubby, and the queen of the kitchen and the court. Her cakes are legendary, her samoosas are always crispy, and when she's on the netball court — nobody's safe. Her energy is unstoppable, her smile is contagious, and she's got one dream: to take her school's netball team to the top.

By her side is Ayanda — tall, graceful, with legs like stilts and a laugh that lights up every hallway. She's Jade's best friend, biggest cheerleader, and the defensive wall no one can break through. Together, they're the perfect team. Until...

Lucy walks back into Jade's life.

The ex-best friend. The ex-netball captain. The one person Jade never thought she'd see again.

After transferring to Jade's high school, Lucy brings more than just her perfect passes and polished ponytail — she brings history. The kind of drama that never really disappears. Suddenly, Jade's stuck in the middle of a messy three-way friendship:

Ayanda doesn't trust Lucy.

Lucy thinks Ayanda's fake.

And Jade? She just wants to play netball, bake cupcakes, and survive high school without losing the people she cares about.

But when netball trials, classroom gossip, team rivalries, and secret crushes all collide — things get complicated. Fast.

Can Jade balance the tension between her oldest friend and her new-old friend?
Will Ayanda and Lucy ever learn to play on the same team — on and off the court?
And what happens when netball isn't just a game anymore... but the only way to hold it all together?
